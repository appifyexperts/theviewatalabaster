Welcome to The View Apartments in Alabaster, Alabama!
Our newly renovated apartments offer modern amenities and stylish touches.

At The View apartments, you’ll find stainless steel appliances, granite countertops, plank-inspired floors, designer paint colors, and much more! Perfectly perched on a ridge of the Appalachian foothills, you’ll delight in breathtaking views and beautiful sunsets when you make your home at our The View.

Explore our 1, 2, & 3-bedroom floor plans and find the perfectly spacious layout best suited to your needs. Plus, you’ll love the community pool, complete with a large sundeck for lounging and relaxing.
